Orange Lemon Jelly

Ingredients

    1 Package Royal Orange Gelatin
    1 Package Royal Lemon Gelatin
    2 Cups Boiling Water
    2 Cups Cold Water

Steps

  • Put Royal Lemon and Orange Gelatins into large bowl
  • Mix well; add boiling water and stir until dissolved; add cold water
  • Pour into molds; chill until firm and server plain or with sliced fruit

    • Bananas are very good. Serves 12.


    Cider Jelly

    Ingredients

      1 Package Royal Orange or Lemon Gelatin
      1/2 Teaspoon Salt
      2 Cups Sweet Cider

    Steps

  • Heat 1 cup cider to boiling
  • Pour over Royal Orange or Lemon Gelatin and salt; stir until dissolved
  • Add 1 cup cold cider
  • Pour into molds and chill until firm

    • As a relish, serve plain. As a dessert, serve plain or with whipped cream. Serves 6.

    Coffee Jelly

    Ingredients

      1 Package Royal Lemon Gelatin
      1 Cup Boiling Water
      1/4 Cups Strong Coffee

    Steps

  • Dissolve Royal Lemon Gelatin in boiling water
  • Add coffee which has been very carefully strained from any coffee grounds
  • Pour into mold; chill until firm

    • Serve with sweetened whipped cream. Serves 6.
